EduTech: Interlinked Platform for National Education Data

This major project involved designing and developing an integrated web platform aimed at synchronizing student data across School, Higher, and Technical Education verticals in India. The core motivation was to overcome the lack of a centralized student database needed for effective educational policy framing and intervention.

The project successfully demonstrates expertise in data integration, Python back-end development (Flask), and the capacity to build systems with significant social and governmental policy implications, resulting in three peer-reviewed publications.

The Policy & Data Problem

  • Gap in Master Database

    Framing targeted education policies is difficult due to the absence of a master database tracking students across various academic levels (school to technical).

  • Objective: Dropout and Grey Area Identification

    The platform's key objective was to enable the identification of dropout percentages (State/District-wise) and grey areas requiring specific resource allocation, providing real-time data for policy makers.
